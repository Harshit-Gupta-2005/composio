## What is Composio?

Composio is an integration platform that connects Box to AI agents and 1000+ other apps — letting you automate workflows, build AI-powered document pipelines, and connect your Box files to tools like Slack, Gmail, Notion, GitHub, and more.

## How to Add Composio to Box

1. Go to [app.composio.dev](https://app.composio.dev)
2. Sign up or log in to your Composio account
3. Search for **Box** in the integrations list
4. Click **Connect** and sign in with your Box account
5. Authorize the requested permissions
6. Done — your Box files are now accessible to any AI agent or workflow you build in Composio

## What You Can Do

Once connected, Composio can:
- Read and download your Box files and folders
- Upload and create new files in Box
- Search across your Box content
- Move, copy, rename, and delete files
- Manage folder structures
- Trigger workflows in 1000+ other apps based on Box activity
